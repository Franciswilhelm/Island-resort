Accomodations

The Island offers a choice of luxurious accommodation options, spread accross the island. You can choose between the resort's beach villas, and the luxurious water villas, which have their own private spa treatment room.

Beach Villas

Two bedrooms, two pools, beach access, personal island host

The resort's most spacious and luxurious beach accommodation, the Beach Suite features private beach access, two private plunge pools, two bedrooms and two open-air bathrooms. The living area's floor-to-ceiling windows illuminate the room with natural light.

Hidden among the tropical greenery on the Island, the beach suites offer a sunrise view or a dramatic sunset view. Private island hosts attend to the needs of Guests staying in this suite.

This suite measures 668 sq. m./ 7,190 sq. ft. and sleeps up to 4 adults and 2 children, or 3 adults and 3 children

Water Villas

The ultimate in a luxury escape, the Water Villa is set in its own private area of the Island. Ride the jet-boat or take the private walkway to this indulgent Private Island hideaway. The villa features two bedrooms, a large infinity pool, a spacious living area and contemporary features and furnishings including a telescope for stargazing.

The villa's unique glass flooring in the living area offers views into the Ocean below. The circular rotating bed in the master bedroom enables guests to have a 270-degree view of the ocean and follow the breath-taking sunset as it descends over the horizon.

The ultimate Maldivian experience, guests staying in the Water Villa also benefit from the service of a personal island host at any time. This villa measures 451 sq. m./ 4,865 sq. ft. and sleeps up to 4 adults and 2 children, or 3 adults and 3 children.
